# Nuclear Physics Notes

Here I share educational material and tutorials on experimental nuclear physics, detector systems, and data analysis.

## Topics

### Gamma Ray Spectroscopy
Introduction to gamma-ray spectroscopy, detector calibration, and peak identification.

### HPGe and Clover Detectors
Working principles of high-purity germanium detectors and the INGA Clover array.

### Compton Scattering
Theory of Compton scattering and experimental spectra.

### Escape Peaks and Detector Effects
Explanation of escape peaks, backscatter peaks, and annihilation peaks in gamma spectra.

### Python for Spectroscopy
Data analysis tools for peak fitting, Gaussian models, and efficiency correction.

### Nuclear Structure Models
Notes on collective excitations, triaxial deformation, and the Davydov model.
